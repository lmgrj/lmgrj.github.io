Who I Am

About Me

I am a doctoral researcher in Computer Science at the Université de Picardie Jules Verne (UPJV), Amiens, affiliated with the MIS Laboratory (Modélisation, Information & Systèmes). My thesis is part of the DemoES Ap.Rés project — a national initiative on networked learning — where I apply AI and machine learning to adaptive educational systems.

I hold a Master's degree in Data Mining & Business Intelligence and have hands-on expertise in deep learning, computer vision, and NLP. Passionate about open science and knowledge sharing.
